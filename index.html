About Mania

A unique artistic journey through music, visual arts, and philosophy

Pioneering Performance Artist

Mania Rasstegaeva is a pioneering performance artist who transforms historical narratives into emotionally compelling theatrical experiences. Her work exists at the intersection of classical opera, historical storytelling, and contemporary performance art, challenging conventional approaches to cultural education.

With over 300 performances of her unique urban performance project, Mania has developed a distinctive methodology that prioritizes emotional connection over academic distance. She deliberately breaks down barriers to "high culture" through warmth, humor, and genuine passion, making historically significant content accessible to diverse audiences.

Her performances combine operatic singing with theatrical storytelling, creating intimate experiences that humanize historical figures and transform potentially intimidating cultural material into relatable, engaging encounters with the past.

Artistic Evolution

Mania's performance practice has undergone significant evolution from solo urban performances to more sophisticated theatrical productions. Her work deliberately occupies the liminal space between formal culture and contemporary accessibility, creating bridges between academic knowledge and emotional resonance, classical art and modern sensibilities, historical figures and present-day audiences.
