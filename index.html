About Mania

A unique artistic journey through music, visual arts, and philosophy

Multi-Disciplinary Artist

Mania Rasstegaeva is a classically trained soprano whose artistic practice transcends traditional boundaries. With formal education spanning classical singing, visual arts, and philosophy, she creates performances that are both intellectually rigorous and emotionally profound.

Education & Training

  • Moscow State Academic Art Institute (Surikov Institute)
    Painting and Drawing, Class of 2017
  • Moscow State University of Culture and Arts
    Classical Singing, Class of 2016
  • RUDN University
    Philosophy, Class of 2008

Artistic Philosophy

Her work explores the deep connections between Russian cultural heritage and contemporary artistic expression. Through collaborations with traditional instrumentalists and innovative arrangements, she preserves folk traditions while making them accessible to modern audiences.
